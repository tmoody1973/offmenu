# LLM Narratives - Raw Idea

**Feature Name**: LLM Narratives

**Description**: AI-generated tour descriptions and restaurant narratives for Food Tour Butler. Uses Groq (Llama 3.3 70B) to generate engaging tour themes, restaurant descriptions, and culinary storytelling. Creates personalized narratives based on the tour's restaurants, cuisine types, awards, and user preferences. Makes the food tour feel like a curated experience with a story arc.
